Caveats (this is a single-rule MVP)

  • Single rule with hardcoded thresholds (z > +2σ → 0% equity, z < −1σ → 100% equity, else 50/50). Multi-rule comparison + White's Reality Check deferred to v10.1.
  • Look-ahead audit: weight in force during month t uses z observed at end of month t−1; verified by test_run_backtest_no_lookahead.
  • Transaction cost = 10bps round-trip per rebalance with |Δw| > 1%. Real-world costs could be higher in early decades.
  • Risk-free rate: Shiller long-rate × 0.6 (short-rate approximation across full sample). The spec calls for FRED DGS3MO 1934+; the single-splice MVP is documented in REVIEW_PACKAGE_v10.0.md §8.
  • Sharpe CIs from stationary block bootstrap (Politis-Romano 1994, mean block length 12 months, 10,000 reps).
  • Strategy and benchmark Sharpe CIs overlap heavily — at these thresholds, MVCI's signal does not produce statistically distinguishable risk-adjusted return outperformance. The value shows up as substantial drawdown reduction.
