About Mean Reversion

CMV-style indicator: real (CPI-adjusted) S&P 500 composite plotted on a log scale, with the long-run log-linear trend fit through the entire 1871–present history. The valuation reading is the percent deviation of the current real price from the fitted trend. Distinct from bi_spx_proxy (SPX/GDP); this variant is purely autoregressive against the price's own long-run trajectory.
