
# MODEL CARD — Quant Engine v50

## Model Details
- **Model Name**: Systematic US Equity Factor Pipeline
- **Version**: v50
- **Type**: Long-only factor-based equity strategy with risk overlay
- **Rebalance**: Monthly (last trading day of month)
- **Developer**: Internal quant research
- **Date**: 20260520_1951
- **Framework**: Python 3.14.3

## Intended Use
- **Primary Use**: Systematic US equity allocation for long-term investors
- **Target Audience**: Institutional allocators ($1M+) and retail investors ($10K+)
- **Rebalance Frequency**: Monthly
- **Holding Period**: Designed for 1+ year horizons
- **Not Intended For**: 
  - Intraday trading
  - Short-only or market-neutral strategies  
  - Leverage > 1.5x
  - Private equity or illiquid markets

## Strategies Included
1. **DD-TARGET** — Quadruple-brake risk overlay (Sharpe 1.069, MaxDD -16.74%)
2. **LowBeta** — Cost-resilient defensive anchor (Sharpe 0.957, beta 0.386)
3. **LowRisk** — Defensive 3-factor blend (Sharpe 0.956)
4. **FACTOR-ONLY** — Transparent no-ML benchmark (Sharpe 0.963)
5. **ENS-Ultra** — ML-augmented growth sleeve (CAGR 13.03%)
6. **ETF-ROTATION** — State machine with HY credit signal (GFC +0.475)
7. **EW** — Equal-weight S&P 500 benchmark
8. **SPY** — Passive buy-and-hold reference

## Data Inputs
- **Universe**: S&P 500 Current & Past watchlist (~1,890 symbols including delisted)
- **Data Source**: Norgate US Equities Diamond (survivorship-bias-free)
- **Price Adjustment**: Total return (splits + dividends)
- **Reference Data**: VIX (1990+), VIX3M (2007+), HYG (2007+), LQD (2002+), US10Y
- **PIT Compliance**: All features lagged by 1 bar vs execution
- **Delisted Handling**: Last-quoted date tracking, returns applied

## Features Used
- Momentum: 252d / 126d skip-1mo, 52-week high nearness, trend consistency
- Risk: realized vol 63d, idiosyncratic vol 63d, negative beta
- Residual momentum (post market-beta adjustment)
- Liquidity: Amihud illiquidity measure
- Trend: above 200-SMA signal

## Training & Validation
- **ML Model**: LightGBM Gradient Boosting
- **Walk-Forward**: 36mo train / 6mo validate / 10d purge / 6mo test, 3mo step
- **OOS Folds**: ~51 independent 6-month windows
- **Random Seed**: 42 (fixed for reproducibility)

## Performance (26-year backtest, 2000-2026, 15bps cost)
- **Top Sleeve (DD-TARGET)**: Sharpe 1.069, CAGR 12.17%, MaxDD -16.74%
- **Benchmark (SPY)**: Sharpe 0.501, CAGR 8.07%, MaxDD -55.19%
- **Edge over SPY**: +113% Sharpe, 3.3x lower drawdown

## Known Limitations
1. **Long-only constraint**: Cannot profit from declining markets
2. **Monthly frequency**: Not suitable for rapid regime changes
3. **US equity only**: No geographic or asset class diversification
4. **GFC weakness**: DD-TARGET Sharpe -1.096 in 2007-2009 systemic crisis
5. **Pre-2007 data**: Missing VIX3M, HYG → DD-TARGET uses 3 of 4 brakes
6. **ML dependency (ENS-Ultra)**: Requires clean training data, retraining
